*Registration Fee and Sponsorship Levels:

The base conference fee is $85 USD (about $125 CAD). If you or your company wishes to contribute beyond the base fee, we offer four sponsorship levels: Bronze ($300 USD), Silver ($1000 USD), Gold ($2500 USD), and Perl ($5000 USD).

For further information on sponsoring YAPC, contact Kevin Lenzo. Any sponsorship in excess of costs will go to other projects of Yet Another Society.
