Standard Talk: Towards a Perl Refactoring Tool                                                                        
                                                                                                                      
The Smalltalk Refactoring Browser is the inspiration for PREFACE, a                                                   
personal research project into writing Refactoring tools for                                                          
Perl. This talk discusses the techniques I used to cross the                                                          
'Refactoring Rubicon' by implementing a pure Perl 'Extract Method'                                                    
refactoring.                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                      
Level: Definitely Scary, by its very nature, PREFACE gets pretty cosy                                                 
with Perl's internal structure. It's all stuff worth knowing though.
