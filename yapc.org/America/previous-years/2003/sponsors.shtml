Please Contribute!

You or your company can choose to sponsor the YAPC and the Yet Another Society by becoming a sponsor -- contributing more than the basic cost. And contributions are tax deductible, as YAS is a non-profit, 501(3)(c) corporation.

Sponsors are listed among the materials, and in the proceedings; they help us keep the costs down for most people's registration through patronage.

If you or your company would like to sponsor YAPC::NA 2003, or to find out more, please contact Kevin Meltzer at kevinm@yetanother.org.
