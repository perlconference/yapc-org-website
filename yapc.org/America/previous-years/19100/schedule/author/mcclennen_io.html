Programming with IO::

Michael McClennen

Internet Public Library

Thursday, 11:00 AM in Rangos III.

See http://www.ipl.org/talks/yapc-using-io/ .

The IO:: libraries seem mysterious to many, especially those who started programming in the early days of Perl.  I will start with some basic examples, demonstrating how easy they are to use in simple situations.  I will then proceed to more advanced use, including pipes and forking, and finish by showing how to build your own IO:: classes for specialized jobs.
