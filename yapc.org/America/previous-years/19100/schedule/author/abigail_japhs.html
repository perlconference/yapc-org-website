JAPHs and other obscure signatures

Abigail

Thursday, 11 AM in Rangos I.

In this presentation, we will explain what JAPHs are and how they came
about. We will analyze a handful of JAPHs to see how they work, gaining
some insight in the flexibility of how Perl operates. We will also show
how to construct a JAPH.

The presentation is currently online at
http://www.foad.org/%7Eabigail/Perl/Talks/Japhs/
