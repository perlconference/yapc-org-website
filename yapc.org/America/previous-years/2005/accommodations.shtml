Hotel

An official hotel has not been selected at this time. An announcement will be made as soon as one is chosen. owever, there are an unlimited (~ 25,000) number of hotel rooms of all prices (USD$40-USD$200/night) within easy walking distance. A "recommended" hotel nearby the conference will be identified and negotiated with for group rates closer to the date of the conference with some already in mind.

Dorms

Excellent dorm facilities are available from many different colleges within the University of Toronto. We are currently negotiating with several to find the best one for YAPC use. Any dorm location that is chosen will be of good quality, very inexpensive (approximately US$40/night/person) and very nearby the on-campus site of the conference.
