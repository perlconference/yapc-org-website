DoubleTree Guest Suites

The DoubleTree Guest Suites of Boca Raton have agreed to give a special room rate for YAPC attendees.

Hotel provides the ideal location. Conveniently located between Ft. Lauderdale and Palm Beach International Airports in the Arvida Corporate Park. Minutes to Boca Raton's finest beaches, world class shopping, dining, golf and tennis. Tropical Courtyard Setting with lushly landscaped oversized pool area. All suites include private living room and bedroom, microwave, refrigerator and coffee maker and our famous chocolate chip cookies upon arrival. Now offering wireless high speed internet access.

The room rate is $59USD (plus tax) for up to 4 people in a suite. All rooms are suites! There is a complimentary breakfast (in case you want to eat before the breakfast at YAPC), free shuttle service to places within 5 miles (including the FAU campus), beer & wine Happy Hour Monday to Friday from 5:30-7:00pm, free parking, complimentary pass to Bally's Fitness, heated pool, whirlpool, wireless access (may be a charge), on-site restaurant and bar, and they are a part of the Hilton Honors program. It is within a few miles of the FAU campus, and 25 minutes from both Palm Beach Internation and Fort Lauderdal airports.

The address is:

701 NW 53rd Street Boca Raton, FL 33487

It is right off of Yamato Rd. near I-95. Their phone number is:

1-561-997-9500

Or, you can call any DoubleTree reservation line. Mention you are going to the "YAPC Perl Conference" to make sure you get the special rate. If you plan on arriving Saturday, or earlier, you may want to call directly to see if they will give you a super-special rate! But, these are great rates for a suite, especially if you double, triple or quad the occupancy.
