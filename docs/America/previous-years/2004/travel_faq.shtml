Travel FAQ

How do I get to the University at Buffalo?

UB's north campus is located in Amherst, NY, about 15 miles Northeast of downtown Buffalo. UB's web site can help plan a trip to campus. UB can also be found in MapQuest.

How do I get to the University Inn?

The University Inn has location information on their website. The University Inn also provides a shuttle to and from the Buffalo airport.

Where on UB's campus is the conference being held?

YAPC events will be held in UB's Natural Sciences Complex.

How do I get from the University Inn to UB's Natural Sciences Complex

The University Inn is approximately one mile Northeast of UB's Natural Sciences Complex. You can drive, walk, or use the complimentary shuttle service.

Where do I go/park on campus?

We have arranged to have two lots on campus reserved during the conference: Governors Lots D and E. If you park in these lots, you will not require a parking tag and should not be ticketed. However, if you park elsewhere on campus, you will require a visitors parking tag and you will be ticketed, so be careful!

How do I get around Buffalo?

  • The University Inn's shuttle can take you to/from the airport and the conference.
