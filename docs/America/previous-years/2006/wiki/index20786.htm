Reminder

I am posting the slides here as a gift to the YAPC attendees. Please do not distribute the slides to anyone else.

Because I can make a living teaching classes, I can also come give the class at YAPC and to go speak to Perl Mongers groups. I pay for this out of my own pocket.

If you distribute the slides elsewhere, you might impede me from making a living giving classes. Then I wouldn't be able to afford to come back to YAPC next year to give more classes for free.

So please enjoy them, but don't distribute them.

Thanks.
