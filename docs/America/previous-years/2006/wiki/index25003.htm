Arriving:

If you are arriving by plane, and not renting a car:

O’Hare (ORD) is the larger airport located on the north-west side of town. The Blue ‘L’ line starts at O’Hare.

  • Take it to the Clark/Lake station
  • Transfer to the Green ‘L’ line going southbound (trains marked either East 63rd or Ashland/63rd).
  • Get off at the 35th-Bronzeville-IIT stop.

Midway (MDW) is a smaller airport located south-west of the city center. The Orange ‘L’ line starts at Midway.

  • You can take it to the Roosevelt stop
  • Transfer to a southbound Green ‘L’ line (trains marked either East 63rd or Ashland/63rd).
  • Go south to the 35th-Bronzeville-IIT stop.

At either station you should buy a CTA visitor's pass or transit card before boarding the train. The machines at these 2 locations accept credit and debit cards.

If you are arriving by car (or renting one from an airport):

  • From O'Hare, take the Kennedy Expressway (I-90) to the the Dan Ryan Expressway (I-90/94), heading south or east. (Google map directions)
  • From Midway, take the Stephenson Expressway (I-55) to the Dan Ryan Expressway (I-90/94), heading north or east. (Google map directions)

From the southbound Dan Ryan:

  • Exit at 31st Street
  • Continue south to 33rd Street
  • Turn left (east).

Metered parking is located along Federal Street north and south of 33rd Street, and in the Visitor's Parking Lot (Lot A4) at 32nd Street and State Street.

If you are arriving by bus or train:

Union Station and the Greyhound station are both not too far from the Roosevelt L station, but too far to walk; take a cab to that station, and board a southbound Green line train (trains marked either East 63rd or Ashland/63rd). Go south to the 35th-Bronzeville-IIT stop.

If you do not have a CTA transit card or visitor's pass, you can purchase one at the 24-hour Jewel supermarket located directly south of the Green line L station, on Roosevelt and Wabash. Union Station has a card vending machine, in the Metra Ticket Concourse, that is open daily 5am - 12:30am.

Checking In:

Early registration for the conference will be held on Sunday June 25th from 5:00 p.m. until 10:00 p.m. in the NORTH State Street Village dorm. There will be a YAPC representative in the lobby of the dorm during those hours with conference packets and a registration list.

Standard registration begins at 7:30 a.m. on Monday June 26th in the lobby of the Herman Hall. The registration center will be fully manned until around 9:00 a.m. when the conference officially begins. After that time, the registration table will be running short staffed during conference hours, so be sure to register first thing Monday morning.

Arrival Dinner:

The Arrival Dinner is at 7 pm at the Goose Island Brewing Company on 3535 North Clark.

We shall gather at the McCormick Tribune Campus Center's south entrance on 33rd street to depart at 6 pm Sunday. This building is immediately north of SSV ("State Street Village" dorms) and west of MSV ("McCormick Student Village" dorms), directly beneath the elevated train tracks.

If you arrive after the main group has left:

  • Board the Green line stop on the campus, and take a north-bound train (labelled "Harlem/Lake") to the very next station, Roosevelt.
  • Get off the Green line and walk to the Red line platform, and board a north-bound Red line train (labelled "Howard").
  • Get off at the Addison station.
  • Head towards Wrigley Field to the west, to Clark street
  • Turn left, heading south, away from the stadium.

Returning from the Arrival Dinner:

  • Take the Red line south-bound (trains marked "95th/Dan Ryan").
  • Transfer to the Green line at Roosevelt, and take a train going southbound (trains marked either East 63rd or Ashland/63rd).
  • Get off at the 35th-Bronzeville-IIT stop.
  • Exit the north end of the platform, and you will get off the station directly behind the SSV dorm.
