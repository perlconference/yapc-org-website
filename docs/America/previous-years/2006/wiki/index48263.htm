The Chicago Fire soccer team happen (note the European-style singular case) to be playing Sunday and Wednesday in their new home, Toyota Park.

  • Sunday 6:00 PM New York Red Bulls
  • Wednesday 8:00 PM Real Salt Lake

Unfortunately they no longer play at Soldier Field on The Lake. Their brand new home is at 7000 S. Harlem, Bridgeview, IL. They don't list bus or EL options in their directions, so if you're determined to go, you'd have to

  • Rent a car.
  • Take a taxi (probably > $30 each way).
  • Take Metra Southwest Service from Union Station to Ashburn or Oak Lawn and a cab from there. (ea. way $2.50 + ~10 for taxi) Check the schedule for limited times, particularly coming back.
  • Take the Orange Line EL to Midway Airport and take a taxi from there (assuming you can get a cab from the city to the suburb of Bridgeview) (ea. way $2 + ~20 for taxi).

See Chicago Fire or call 888-MLS-FIRE for details.
