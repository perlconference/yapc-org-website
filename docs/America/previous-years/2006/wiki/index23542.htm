BOFs

Could someone who knows the facilities please put together a BOF scheduling page, so BOF hosts can sign up ASAP for (non-conflicting) times in the available venues? E.g., 5:15 in the SE corner of the ballroom, on the lawn under the big oak tree, etc. It would also be nice to know which venues have whiteboards, projectors, seats, etc. -TimMaher

BOFs are Birds of a Feather sessions. It is an old nickname for meetings and talks that are off the main schedule at conferences. They can be held at anytime a group wants to meet and talk about a subject. They can be led by a speaker or be a rambling multithreaded conversation. Post your BOF here and announce a time/place to meet at YAPC (meal breaks, early evening are common times).
