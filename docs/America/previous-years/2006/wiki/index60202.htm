There are plans on having a post-YAPC hack-a-thon for Pugs/Perl 6. Even if you aren't a Perl 6 hacker right now, you can still help out by working on things like documentation and test cases. According to Audrey, you can even code in Perl 5 for the Pugs/Perl 6 project, and Adam will take Perl 5 helpers for the PITA smokers, or even people that just want to install operating systems for 3 days :)

Consider sticking around for a few days after the conference to help out.

Possibly one of the Perl 6 or Pugs people could list out some specifics that might be addressed after YAPC.
