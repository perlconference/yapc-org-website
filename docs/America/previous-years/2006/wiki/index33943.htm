Network problems

Regarding the problems we had today with the wireless network, we had to tell the access points we set up at the conference hall to stay on channels 1 and 6 respectively, instead of hopping to find the "best" one. This made everyone get continual drop-offs. The other problem is that the IT guys at IIT only gave us a class C network, which meant that at best, only 253 people were going to be given DHCP leases. As you can guess, we ran out of IPs. The network guys have been notified, and hopefully they'll be nice to us and allocate more IPs.
