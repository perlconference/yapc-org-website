By Plane

Chicago is served by O'Hare and Midway airports. Almost every major airline runs some service to at least one of these airports. Each airport is connected to major expressways and the Chicago 'L', making getting from the airport to the conference and back easy.

  • O'Hare http://www.flychicago.com/ohare/home.asp
  • Midway http://www.flychicago.com/midway/home.asp

Use American Airlines discount code A2566AN for 5% off tickets booked at aa.com, which may or may not be your cheapest option.

Directions

From Midway

    • Public Transportation:
      • Take the CTA Orange Line to the Roosevelt station. (The Orange line ends in Midway, so it's impossible to take this train in the wrong direction)
      • Exit the train on the left and walk across the platform to the south-bound trains.
      • Take the southbound CTA Green Line
      • Exit 35th-Bronzeville-IIT station.

There are some useful maps and visitor resources available for free in Midway. Just after you come down the escalator onto the baggage claim level (which you will probably do as you walk to the Orange line), there is a half-round desk to your right by the men's room. It is usually fairly well stocked with maps and advertisements geared toward visitors. Grab a CTA map if you want. They're handy.

    • Driving:
      • Exit the airport and travel North on Cicero Avenue.
      • Turn right onto the Stevenson Expressway North (I-55).
      • From the Stevenson, exit to the Dan Ryan Expressway East (I-90/94).
      • Take the Dan Ryan Expressway east to 31st Street exit.
      • Continue south to 33rd Street, turn left (east).

Metered parking is located along Federal Street north and south of 33rd Street, and in the Visitor's Parking Lot (Lot A4) at 32nd Street and State Street. Map

From O'Hare

    • Public Transportation:
      • Take the CTA Blue Line to the Clark station.
      • Exit the train and follow the signage to the CTA Green Line trains.
      • Take the CTA Green Line bound for Ashland-63rd or 63rd-Cottage Grove to the 35th-Bronzeville-IIT station.
    • Driving.
      • Take the Kennedy Expressway (I-90) south. It will merge into I-94 and become the Dan Ryan Expressway (I-90/94).
      • Take the Dan Ryan Expressway east to 31st Street exit
      • Continue south to 33rd Street, turn left (east).

Metered parking is located along Federal Street north and south of 33rd Street, and in the Visitor's Parking Lot (Lot A4) at 32nd Street and State Street. Map
