Off-Site Accomodation

Club Quarters

Club Quarters is the recommended off-site accomodation. There are two Club Quarters locations in town. The one that we recommend is the Wacker and Michigan location. The hotel is a few blocks north of the Green ‘L’ line, which will take you to IIT. To book at Club Quarters, visit the hotel website (http://www.clubquarters.com/) and login with the password ‘illinois institute of technology’. Alternately, you can call 212-575-0006 or in Europe 44 (0) 20-7666-1616.

To get here from O'Hare, take the Blue Line L downtown, get out at either Clark and Lake or Washington, get to street level, and take a cab for $4-$6. Alternatively, transfer to the Red Line at Washington (look for a staircase going down once you get off the Blue Line, take the tunnel over to the Red Line) and go north towards Lake. You can either get off at lake and walk several blocks north/northwest, or you can get out at Grand (the next stop), stop for a beer at Rock Bottom Brewery, and then walk south/southeast several blocks. –KentCowgill

The 111 Adams is also convienent to the train and less of a walk from the blue (O'Hare) trains.
