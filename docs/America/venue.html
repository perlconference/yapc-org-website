About the Venue

The University of Houston provides conference facilities within the University Center, a four-level facility with numerous meeting rooms, a game room (can be rented by the hour), and various food venues. A map of the facility is available. The university also provides a photo tour.

If you need any help or have any questions, please contact help-na at yapc.org
