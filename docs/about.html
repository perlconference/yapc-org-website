About Perl Conferences

Getting Your Event Listed Here

If you're holding a Perl event, we'd love to list it here on the Perl Conferences site. The main page events are data from The Perl Review Perl Community Events Calendar in Google calendar, and the community. To get your event added, send email to pr -at- perlfoundation -dot- org or contact Mark Keating.
